Risotto

Description

Risotto is a creamy and savory Italian rice dish that is known for
its luxurious texture. The signature creaminess comes from the high-starch
content of specific short-grain rices, such as Arborio or Carnaroli, which
release their starches when cooked and stirred slowly in a flavorful broth.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Broth
  • Arborio Rice
  • White Wine
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Black Pepper
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Add broth to a pot and bring it to a simmer so that it's ready
    for our risotto. After heating it until steaming, reduce to low heat,
    cover, and keep warm.
  2. Melt butter in a large skillet before adding onion and salt.
    Cook, stirring occasionally until soft and translucent. Then, toast
    the rice for a few minutes in the fat and aromatics until lightly
    toasted and translucent around the edges.
  3. Then add wine and stir to combine until the liquid is absorbed
  4. Add three-quarters of the broth, cover and simmer for 10-15min
    until the rice is just al dente. Once you've reached this stage uncover
    and add the remaining broth 1/2 cup at a time while stirring
    until it's absorbed and the rice is completely tender
  5. Remove from heat, and garnish with parmesan and pepper
