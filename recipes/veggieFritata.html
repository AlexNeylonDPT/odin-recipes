Veggie Fritata

Description

You could say that a fritata is similar to a quiche but with no crust
It's an egg dish or an egg-based "pie" that works well for breakfast,
brunch, lunch, dinner, or even sliced thinly for an appetizer.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Zucchini
  • Green Onions
  • Broccoli
  • Kosher Salt & Black Pepper
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Baking Powder
  • Milk
  • Feta Cheese
  • Fresh Parsley & Thyme

Steps

  1. Roast the veggies on a sheet pain in the oven at 450 degrees
    Farenheit for 15-minutes, reduce temp to 400.
  2. Whisk the eggs with a dash of baking powder, milk, feta, thyme
    parsley, salt and pepper. When the veggies are ready add them to the
    batter and mix to combine.
  3. Heat up a little olive oil in a cast iron skillet. Add the egg and
    veggie mixture to the pan and cook on the stove top over medium-high
    heat for 3 minutes or until the bottom of the eggs settle, then
    transfer to the middle rack of the oven to finish cooking.
    Watch for the center of the fritata to fully set (about 8 min).
